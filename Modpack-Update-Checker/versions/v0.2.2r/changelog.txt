📢 Update v0.2.2r

⚠️ Important Notice

Old worlds may get corrupted due to the changes introduced in v0.2.0r. I haven’t personally encountered crashes yet, but on old worlds the ocean may be laggier and you might notice sharp borders where new ocean chunks generate.

📝 About This Update

Changed max render distance from 53 to 64

Fixed some ice and fire mobs having a error for icon on map.

Fixed some items becoming invisible in hotbar.

Improved fps and fps stability a little bit.

Improved world gen speed by 2x thanks to [FORK] Twilight Forest Thread Safety Addon lets hope no more world gen crashes or i have to revert this

The modpack is now on [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/modpack/create-kryp).
